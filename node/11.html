About Me

I am a videographer currently living in Eugene, Oregon. Oregon has been my home for my entire life and I couldn't think of a better place to live than the northwest. I graduated from the University of Oregon with a degree in Journalism and Communication in 2016 with a Bachelor’s of Science, Cum Laude. I have worked with clients from OPB, Travel Oregon, and several local musicians in creating and telling exciting stories. Currently, I work for OR Media, a professional media company based within the School of Journalism and Communication at the University of Oregon. We specialize in providing visual documentation for educational based grant projects.

I believe everyone has an interesting story to tell and multimedia journalism allows viewers to see a small slice of a person's life in an engaging way.

On top of producing journalistic documentaries, I love the art of lighting. Understanding light and shadows is key in visual storytelling.
